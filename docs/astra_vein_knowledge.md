## WHAT CONDITIONS DO YOU TREAT
We treat varicose veins, spider veins, venous insufficiency, venous stasis ulcers and wound care, hand veins, foot veins, circulation problems, and deep vein thrombosis or D-V-T.

## WHAT TREATMENTS DO YOU OFFER
We offer endovenous laser ablation, radiofrequency ablation, sclerotherapy, and microphlebectomy.

## SPIDER VEINS
Yes, we treat spider veins with sclerotherapy. It's very effective.

## VARICOSE VEINS
We treat varicose veins with several methods: endovenous laser ablation, radiofrequency ablation, or microphlebectomy, depending on your case.

## FIBROID TREATMENT
We offer minimally invasive outpatient procedures for uterine fibroids. These help with heavy periods, pelvic pain, and fertility issues.

## VENOUS INSUFFICIENCY
Yes, we specialize in treating venous insufficiency. This is when blood doesn't flow properly back to the heart. We can help with that.

## DEEP VEIN THROMBOSIS
We treat deep vein thrombosis, or D-V-T. This is a blood clot in a deep vein. Please see Dr. Bolotin for evaluation and treatment.

## WOUND CARE
Yes, we provide wound care for venous stasis ulcers and other vein-related wounds.
